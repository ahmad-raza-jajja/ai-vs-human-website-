What is this project?

Welcome to "AI vs Human" - an interactive and educational website created for the CS Girlies Hackathon 2025! This project explores the fascinating differences between artificial intelligence and human intelligence across multiple dimensions.

As AI continues to advance and integrate into our daily lives, understanding the unique strengths and limitations of both AI and human intelligence becomes increasingly important.

Comparing AI & Human Intelligence

Explore how artificial and human intelligence compare across different dimensions:

Speed & Computation

AI

Can process billions of calculations per second. Excels at data processing, pattern recognition, and solving well-defined problems.

Human

Slower computational speed but unmatched efficiency in energy usage. The human brain uses only ~20 watts of power while performing remarkable feats.

Creativity & Innovation

AI

Can generate art, music, and writing by analyzing and recombining existing patterns. Lacks intrinsic understanding of its creations.

Human

Possesses original creativity driven by emotions, experiences, and intentionality. Can create truly novel ideas and concepts.

Learning & Adaptation

AI

Learns through vast amounts of data and explicit training. Specialized for specific domains with limited transfer learning abilities.

Human

Can learn from minimal examples and transfer knowledge across domains. Excels at intuitive learning and adaptation to new situations.

Emotions & Consciousness

AI

Can simulate emotional responses but lacks genuine emotional experiences. No evidence of consciousness or self-awareness.

Human

Experiences rich emotional states that influence decision-making and creativity. Possesses consciousness and self-awareness.
